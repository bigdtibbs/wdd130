Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to drive internet traffic to our new and improved website which will provide information about available white water rafting experiences through our company - Dry Oar White Water Rafting Company. Both new and existing customer will find promotion material about this exciting hobby that will generate interest and encourage them to select an adventure that suits their liking. The website will also create general awareness of the hobby of white water rafting and provide information about about the history of the hobby, recommendations about safety precautions that should be taken, and some of the best times of year to enjoy the hobby. The website will also have a means where customers can purchase a rafting experience, make reservations with a river guide, find accomodations near the river, and plan out their river rafting adventure. This will be supplemented with pictures and videos of other adventures that will provide insipration for the customer to help them visualize their own adventure. Lastly, the website will have a means for the customer to reach out to Dry Oar White Water Rafting Company for assistance with questions, concerns, reservations, and any other reason that the customer may need to contact the company.

Audience

Anyone and everyone that can fit into a life jacket is invited to participate in the exitement of white water rafting. We have adventures available for families, scouting groups, coorporate groups, bachelor/bachelorette parties, birthday parties, and everyone interested in participating. Our site will appeal to both the enthusiast and the beginner alike. Because our company has guides that are expertly skilled and trained, we are able to accomodate the beginner up to the individual who has been down the hardest river. We have locations and permits on several rivers, along with the equipment to handle large groups and small groups. The equipment also allows us to accomodate the wide range of skill levels. If an individual has a desire to try the hobby and they have access to a mobile device, laptop, tablet, or desktop computer, our site will help them find the adventure that suits their interest. Because our website will be available on all platforms, any customer should be able to search for and find our company. The combination of availibility to group size, equipment, rivers and permits, skilled guides, and a website that will function on all platforms will target the largest audience possible and should drive new and existing customers to our business.
